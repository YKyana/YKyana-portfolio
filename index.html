ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Yana

I'm a Hard Surface Artist specializing in modeling hard surfaces and creating clean, optimized game assets.

I enjoy taking a simple shape and turning it into a finished asset that looks great and performs well in-game. I work with Maya, Blender, Substance 3D Painter, Marmoset Toolbag, and Adobe Photoshop.

I'm currently focusing on detailing and optimization, and I'm also diving deeper into Blender. I want to keep developing in this direction and create even higher quality assets.
